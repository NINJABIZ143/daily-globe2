When Diet Culture Becomes Dangerous

Extreme dieting trends are spreading rapidly through social media platforms, often promoted by influencers and celebrities without medical supervision. Experts warn that these behaviors can severely impact both physical and mental health.

Health Experts Warn:
Diets that completely eliminate food groups or replace meals with supplements may lead to nutrient deficiencies, hormonal imbalance, and long-term damage.

Medical professionals emphasize the importance of evidence-based nutrition and encourage young people to question online health claims that promise instant results without consequences.
